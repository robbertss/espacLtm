Smipack FW500e Flow Wrapping Machine

/ Description /


See Video

The new FW series of automatic flow wrapping machines produce in Italy by SMIPACK are designed with Industry 4.0 and IoT (Internet of Things) technologies in mind. This line features a rotary bar sealing system and includes seven models—five with an upper film reel (three of which are tailored for entry-level needs) and two with a lower film reel. These machines are ideal for packaging a wide variety of small or irregularly shaped products, whether they arrive loose (as single units) or pre-grouped (as multipacks). Depending on the model, the machines can achieve speeds of up to 200 packs per minute, using either single-layer or laminated flexible film. The integrated sealing and film-folding systems, along with a dynamic, adjustable conformer, ensure quick and easy setup, offering excellent versatility across the FW range.


The FW500e comes with a 150mm wide sealing bar, a maximum product height of 60mm and an adjustable forming box for multiple product sizes.


Technical Data:


High reel flow pack machine

Sealing system with rotary bar

Sliding surfaces of the products in AISI304

Infeed with adjustable guides and easily removable interlocking vanes

Longitudinal sealing consisting of 2 pairs of drive wheels, the first pair pulls the film and the second does the sealing

Manual opening of the longitudinal sealing wheels

High reel holder with mechanical clutch

Manual closing reel holder shaft

Standard dynamic film conformer with position indicator with numerical display

Cross sealing with bar 150 mm - 5.91” width

Variation of the sealing bar height position by handwheel

Motorized product outfeed conveyor adjustable in height by handwheel

Drawers to collect product waste, under the entire length of the infeed conveyor

Printed film centring device, no product - no bag, and end of film reel sensor included

Motors configuration controlled by inverters: infeed conveyor/longitudinal sealer/ outfeed conveyor with independent asynchronous motors

Motors configuration controlled by drivers: cross sealer with brushless motor

Machine stop in phase
