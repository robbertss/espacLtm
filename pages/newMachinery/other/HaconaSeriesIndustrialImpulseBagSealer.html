Hacona Series Industrial Impulse Bag Sealer

/ Description /


See Video

The I–model industrial sealing machine is designed for heavy duty applications

Do to the very high closing pressure of the sealing bars a perfect sealing quality can be achieved.

he industrial sealing width is 8 mm

The sealing bars close pneumaticly and assure a constant pressure over the complete sealing length

Air tight sealing guaranteed!

basic model can be equiped with 5 different sizes of sealing bars

If a bigger size of bag needs to be sealed it is not necessery to buy a new machine, just change the sealing bars within 2 minutes to a wider size

A further advantage is that if sealing bar elements need to be replaced, then simply install a new set of sealing bars within 2 minutes and the maintenance of the used sealing bar can be carried out easily without stopping your packing process!


Advantages of the machine:


Very fast working cycle

Small space requirement

Low energy consumption

8 mm wide bi-active sealing (upper & lower) for a high quality industrial seal

5.7” LCD Touch Display control unit

User-friendly menu system

Multiple level safety system for high operational safety

Two digital counters (1. absolute cycle counter, 2. working cycle counter (can be reset)

Sealing bars are operated electropneumatically

Very high sealing pressure
