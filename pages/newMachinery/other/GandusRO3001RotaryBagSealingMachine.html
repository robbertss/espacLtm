Gandus RO3001 Rotary Bag Sealing Machine

/ Description /


See Video

High speed rotary heat sealer designed to work with high performances even in the most extreme conditions.

Ideal for packaging a wide range of products from food to chemical, pharmaceutical, small parts and parts.

For all types of heat sealable materials.

Stainless steel and anodized aluminium construction.


Advantages and Benefits

Robust, compact, versatile, reliable and long-lasting heat sealer

Ideal for sealing any bag in flexible heat-sealable material

Minimal and extremely simple maintenance

Equipped with an inkjet printing system

Available with the new motorized support, which allows you to support the machine, adjust it and manage the packaging in an even simpler and more agile way.

Inkjet coding system with HP technology and keyboard to allow printing of messages with a resolution of up to 600 DPI in 12.7 mm, with alphanumeric characters, barcodes and logos.


Technical features

Applications

Food, cosmetics, automotive, electronics, small parts, e-commerce packaging, pellets and agriculture, chemical products

Heat Sealable Materials and Packaging

Ideal for square-bottom bags, the RO 3001 stands out for its extraordinary modularity of use in the presence of different materials, presenting itself as the ideal heat sealer for sealing any bag in heat-sealable flexible material.

Also ideal for packages that require the use of jumpers.


Sealing

Max sealing speed up to 12 m/min.


Operation

Compact and robust machine, designed to work with high performances even in extreme working situations, with long shifts, in difficult environments and with demanding products.

Minimal and extremely simple maintenance.

Designed and built as an independent sealing machine or as a sealing station for automatic bagging lines.

The vertical electrical adjustment of the new motorized support can be performed by acting directly on the control panel.


Coding system equipped with inkjet printing system


Versions

Head only version

With support and integrated tape in the support. Machine and tape synchronized in speed. The height of the tape can be changed manually.

With motorized support, with tape as an option. Machine and tape synchronized in speed. The motorized support allows you to act with simplicity and agility on the height of the machine and the tape, when present.

Equipped with inkjet printing system

Sealing station version to complete automatic bagging lines


Accessories on request

New inkjet thermal marking system

Roller conveyors for loading and unloading bags

Upper bag flap trimmer

Manual air expulsion system contained in the bag

The materials are available in stock in different sizes and thicknesses, or can be ordered on request.

Available neutral or with custom print.
